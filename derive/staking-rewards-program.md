# Staking Rewards Program

The program outlined below and all numbers provided are subject to change.

# Program Purpose

The Staking Rewards Program is designed to reward DRV holders for staking their tokens on Derive. By converting your DRV into stDRV and holding stDRV, you can earn weekly rewards, enjoy fee discounts, and choose flexible unstaking options.

***

# Program Scope

* Staking: Earn rewards when you stake your DRV tokens, which are converted to stDRV at a 1:1 ratio.
* Fee Discounts: In addition to earning rewards, stDRV holders enjoy fee discounts on the platform.

***

# Reward Tokens

* Rewards are paid out in stDRV tokens.
* The weekly reward amount (currently 250,000 stDRV per week, subject to change) is updated regularly and can be viewed at derive.xyz/drv.

***

# Eligibility

* The program is open to all DRV holders, simply stake your DRV to participate.

***

# How To Earn Rewards

* Staking Your DRV: Convert your DRV to stDRV at a 1:1 ratio by staking on the platform.
* Accumulating Rewards: Once you hold stDRV, you will earn weekly rewards proportional to your share of the total staked amount. Rewards are calculated at daily intervals and then aggregated for weekly distribution.

***

# Reward Distribution

* Rewards are calculated, audited, and paid out on a weekly basis.
* Typically, rewards are distributed one week after the reward epoch ends.

***

# Staking Mechanics and Unstake Options

* Staking Conversion: DRV staked is converted into stDRV at a 1:1 ratio.
* Unstake Options:
  * 28-Day Unlock: Unstake your tokens with a 28-day unlock period at a 1:1 ratio.
  * Instant Unlock: Unstake your tokens instantly, subject to a 20% haircut.

***

# Getting Started

1. Stake Your DRV: Visit the staking interface on Derive to convert your DRV to stDRV.
2. Track & Claim Your Rewards: Monitor your staking rewards and claim them at [derive.xyz/drv](https://derive.xyz/drv).
3. Enjoy reduced fees: As a stDRV holder, benefit from reduced fees across the platform.
