# Overview

Derive is a decentralised autonomous organisation (DAO) established to design, build and govern a financial derivatives protocol. In this section, we describe the structure of the DAO and how Derive governance creates a decentralised and self-sustaining ecosystem.

<Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/1caadcb3ca1ad623ced348951422e1ad2168f93af40f988c5c35df7ad561f2cf-Group_427320533.png" />

The DAO consists of five components:

1. [Governance](https://docs.derive.xyz/docs/governance)- the system that facilitates interactions between service providers, the protocol and the treasury.
2. [Token](https://docs.derive.xyz/docs/token) - the ERC20 token that enables decentralised governance of the DAO.
3. [Protocol](https://docs.derive.xyz/docs/overview-2) - the smart contracts that create a self-custodial, decentralised derivatives protocol.
4. [Treasury](https://docs.derive.xyz/docs/treasury) - the funds used to maintain and improve the protocol.
5. [Service Providers](https://docs.derive.xyz/docs/service-providers) - Independent entities that seek funding to maintain and improve the protocol.
